For Sneefee/Lesnee

Posted on 2020-03-20 by sneefee

Hi Sneefee or Lesnee,

I’m admin of Sneeland (fans’ backup version) website. After you left TMNT and deleted accounts, I made this site to exhibit your works I collected. Since I can not contact you for permission, please email to backblackto0#gmail.com if you don’t want this site exhibit your works.

Actually, in 2012 I touched your BNB first time and I love it immediately. At that time, I confused about my gay identity as a middle school student and I was afraid that my families and friends didn’t accecpt me, thought I was freak. So I only could hide my emotion even though I loved some boys.

Through your BNB comic, I learned that I’m not the only gay person in the world and there are many people who accept and tolerate LGBTQ+ just like Splinter in BNB. And I took courage to show my identity to my friends, which let me made many true friends. It really changed my life and made me decide to find a free country for LGBTQ+ to live. I’m working on it.

Anyway, thank you Sneefee/Lesnee! I really hope you can continue creating amazing works, but I also respect your decision. Hope you can protect yourself during COVID-19.

Best wish,

Admin
